# Jupyter Notebook

## Overview

This folder contains the Python notebook used for data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and business insight generation.
The notebook was developed to understand sales performance, customer behavior, regional trends, and profitability before building the Power BI dashboard.

---

## Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Exploratory Data Analysis Performed

### Business KPIs

The following key metrics were calculated:
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Customers

---

### Revenue Analysis

Questions Answered:
- How much revenue has the business generated?
- How has revenue changed over time?
- Which products generate the highest sales?

Analysis Performed:
- Total Sales Calculation
- Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
- Top 10 Products by Sales

---

### Category Analysis

Questions Answered:
- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- Which categories contribute the highest profit?

Analysis Performed:
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category

---

### Regional Analysis

Questions Answered:
- Which regions perform best?
- Which regions generate the highest profit?

Analysis Performed:
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Region

---

### Product Analysis

Questions Answered:
- Which products drive business growth?
- Which products generate the highest profit?

Analysis Performed:
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Top 10 Products by Profit

---

## Key Findings
- Technology was the highest-performing category.
- West region generated the highest revenue and profit.
- Revenue showed strong growth over the observed period.
- A small group of products contributed significantly to overall sales.

---

## Outcome
The insights generated from this notebook were later transformed into an interactive Power BI dashboard for business decision-making.
