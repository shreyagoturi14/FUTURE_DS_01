# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail business sales data and transforming raw data into meaningful business insights using **Python, Jupyter Notebook, and Power BI**.

The objective of this project is to identify sales trends, profitable product categories, top-performing products, and regional business performance through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

##  Project Objectives

This project aims to answer key business questions such as:

- How much revenue has the business generated?
- Which products generate the highest sales?
- Which categories are the most profitable?
- Which regions perform best?
- How has revenue changed over time?
- What insights can support better business decisions?

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Sales Performance
- Total Sales: **$2.30M**
- Total Orders: **5009**
- Total Customers: **793**

### Category Insights
- Technology generated the highest sales.
- Technology generated the highest profit.

### Regional Insights
- West region achieved the highest sales.
- West region generated the highest profit.

### Product Insights
- A small group of products contributed significantly to overall revenue.
- High-value products generated the majority of business profits.

### Trend Analysis
- Revenue showed consistent growth over time.
